Our Projects

52-WSC

52 Weeks Saving Challenge

About

This is a fun and easy way to build savings habits.
The challenge is to save from 1-52 weeks of the year an amount equal to the number of the week. In week 1, save Shs 10,000.
In week 2, save Shs 20,000. In week 3, save Shs 30,000 and so on.
By Week 52, you'll save Shs. 520,000, and your total savings will be Shs. 13,780,000

FSA

Fixed Savings Account

About

A flexible savings account with competitive interest rates, allowing members to save for both short and long-term goals.

Requirements
  • Active MCS membership
  • Minimum opening balance: UGX 50,000
  • Valid identification documents
  • Proof of income

GWC

Generational Wealth Creation

About

A long-term investment program designed to build and preserve wealth for future generations through diversified investments.

Requirements
  • Active MCS membership
  • Minimum investment: UGX 100,000
  • Valid identification documents
  • Financial statement

CGF

Commercial Goat Farming

About

An agricultural investment program focused on commercial goat farming, providing both income and asset growth opportunities.

Requirements
  • Active MCS membership
  • Minimum investment: UGX 200,000
  • Land ownership or lease agreement
  • Agricultural experience preferred

CA

Clubs Account

About

A group savings program where members pool resources to achieve common financial goals through regular contributions.

Requirements
  • Active MCS membership
  • Group of 5-20 members
  • Agreed monthly contribution
  • Group registration form

RSS

Retirement Savings Scheme

About

A long-term retirement planning program offering tax benefits and professional investment management for a secure retirement.

Requirements
  • Active MCS membership
  • Minimum monthly contribution: UGX 50,000
  • Valid identification documents
  • Employment verification
